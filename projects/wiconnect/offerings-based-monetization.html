Ad Based Monetization

WiConnect generates revenue from ads shown to your Wi-Fi users. But this is Wi-Fi advertising reinvented: our ad platform is optimized and integrated into the Real Time Bidding (RTB) eco-system augmented with direct sales, and leverages unique ad formats and capabilities leading to the highest possible ad-based revenue using precise ad targeting methodologies.

Advertising platform optimized and integrated into Real Time Bidding (RTB) eco-system with the following features

  • Premium programmatic management
  • Multi-SSP header bidding
  • AI based data segment identification for hyper-targeting
  • Data gathering with integration with global exchanges
  • IAB compilant ad formats
  • 1st and 3rd party data integration
