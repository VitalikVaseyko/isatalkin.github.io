Careers

WiConnect is headquartered in New York, but our team members are dispersed geographically - talent and determination is what matters to us.

A fully funded startup in a rapidly growing industry, we offer flexibility, a collaborative environment, and the opportunity to directly impact the strategic direction of the company. And of course, all the usual benefits you’d expect.

Who You’ll Be Working With

Our founders have significant expertise in the communications industry, have deployed and monetized cellular and Wi-Fi networks for decades, and led a number of successful subsidiary launches.

Though they know the industry with an impressive network, they don’t have egos – they’re leaders you’ll want to work with.

WiConnect team members are

  • Self-starters and wear multiple hats – proactively solve customer and partner problems, and have the urgency to get the job done

  • Visionary and can see the long-game

  • Enjoy a fun working environment, and contributing to a happy yet productive culture

  • Smart and collaborative, we are hardworking but believe in a work-life balance
